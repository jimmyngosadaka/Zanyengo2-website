Zanyengo Trustee Foundation

Zanyengo Trustee Foundation is a community-focused organization established to support development and welfare in Chasefu and Mambwe Eastern Province. Formed on 5 February, 2024. A dedicated environmental organisation committed to combating climate change and promoting sustainable practices in Zambia. . Zanyengo Trustee Foundation is a youth led organisation registered under PACRA as a non-profit making organization. The organisation operates from Mambwe and Chasefu Districts of Eastern Province.

Zanyengo Trustee Foundation is run by a passionate group of youths determined to make a significant difference in their community. As an organisation, we believe in fostering a sustainable and environmentally conscious community. Through education, innovation, and active participation, we strive to mitigate climate change, promote sustainable practices, and ensure a healthier future for Zambia and beyond.

The organisation focuses on combating climate change through sustainable ways which are afforestation, waste management and forest management. Currently Zanyengo Trustee Foundation has secured a forest place close to 200,000 hectares in Chasefu District and is in the process of completing the formation of the Community Forest management Groups (CFMG). Through this project, Zanyengo intends to work close with the community as a way of securing buy in for their involvement in the protection of the forest. Currently, Zanyengo is also leading on a waste management project in Mambwe District.


Zanyengo Trustee Foundation is a newly formed organisation who had its first encounter with the Zambian Governance Foundation in May 2024 through the review process for the Civil Society Challenge Fund. Despite not being selected for funding, ZGF was impressed with what the organisation had presented. As part of organisational strengthening support, Zanyengo was considered for strategic support in partnership with SGS Consulting, South Africa. The support was aimed at supporting the organisation to build itself strategically and position itself in the climate change sector. An independent consultant was assigned to the organisation to provide guidance and support in the development of the strategic plan that would give them more ground and leverage for further strategic engagement with more funding and non-funding partners and amplify their strategic picture on what they intend to do as an organisation.

Our Services

Education & Training

Climate change, driven primarily by human activities such as fossil fuel burning and deforestation, has significant impacts on both the environment and human health. Deforestation leads to increased greenhouse gases, soil erosion, disrupted water cycles, and loss of biodiversity. ZTF is dedicated to reversing these trends through strategic initiatives aimed at restoring forests and promoting sustainable land use.

Waste managment

Improper waste disposal contributes to climate change and environmental degradation. ZTF will continue advocating for effective waste management practices, including recycling and composting, to minimize these impacts. By educating communities and implementing sustainable waste management systems, ZTF aims to reduce greenhouse gas emissions and promote a cleaner environment.

Agriculture & Sustainability

Smallholder farmer training, inputs, and market access programs.ZTF encourages the adoption of sustaniable farming methods to reduce the environmental impact of chemical fertilizers. By educating farmers on climate smart agricultural practices to improve soil health and increase agricultural productivity.

Youth & Women Empowerment

Mentorship, leadership development, and entrepreneurship support.
