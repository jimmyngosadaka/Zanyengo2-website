Zanyengo Trustee Foundation

Zanyengo Trustee Foundation is a community-focused organization established to support development and welfare in Chasefu district, Eastern Province. Formed on 5 February, 2024, under the leadership of Senior Chief Magodi.

Mission

We empower communities through education, health, and sustainable initiatives.

Vision

A thriving community where every member has opportunity and dignity.

Location

Chasefu district, Eastern Province.

Our People

Led by Senior Chief Magodi with a diverse board and volunteers.

Our Services

Education & Training

We provide scholarships, adult literacy programs, and skill development workshops.

Waste managment

Community health drives, preventive care, and mobile clinic partnerships.

Agriculture & Sustainability

Smallholder farmer training, inputs, and market access programs.

Youth & Women Empowerment

Mentorship, leadership development, and entrepreneurship support.

Impact Statement

Through community partnerships, we strive to deliver measurable improvements in education, health, and livelihoods.

Approach

We work with local leaders, assess needs, and implement scalable programs with clear milestones.
